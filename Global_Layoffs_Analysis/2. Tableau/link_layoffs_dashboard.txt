Tableau Dashboard – Global Layoffs Analysis
( Dashboard : https://public.tableau.com/views/World_Layoffs_Analysis_Dashboard/Dashboard1?:language=en-US&:sid=&:redirect=auth&:display_count=n&:origin=viz_share_link )

This dashboard focuses on exploratory and descriptive analysis of global layoffs
during the COVID-19 pandemic.

Key elements include:
- Industry-wise layoffs distribution
- Funding Impact across company stages
- Year-over-year layoff and geographic layoffs trends
- Interactive filters for year and industry

Purpose:
To identify patterns, trends, and high-impact sectors affected by layoffs
and support exploratory business analysis.
